Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a car with Getaska?

Fill in the booking form above with your name, phone number, service type, pickup location, destination, and preferred date and time. We'll confirm your booking via WhatsApp within minutes and assign a driver to you.

What areas do you serve?

We currently operate in Lagos, covering trips within Lagos as well as airport transfers to and from MMIA and the Domestic Terminal.

How are your drivers verified?

Every Getaska driver goes through identity verification, a valid driver's licence check, a background check, and a driving assessment. Your safety is our top priority.

How do I pay?

We accept cash and bank transfer. Your fare is agreed before the trip starts — no surprises at the end. Payment details are shared when we confirm your booking via WhatsApp.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Plans change — we understand. Simply message us on WhatsApp as early as possible. We ask for at least 2 hours' notice so we can reassign your driver without inconvenience.

Do you provide cars for wedding and events?

Yes! We provide event transportation for weddings, birthday parties, graduations, and corporate events. We can also arrange multiple vehicles for larger events. Contact us early to confirm availability for your date.
